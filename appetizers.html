Broccoli Cheddar Bites

Ingredients

  • Head of broccoli
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and spray a mini muffin pan.
  2. Blend head of broccoli and add cheese, flour, seasonings, and eggs.
  3. Stir mixture until combined.
  4. Add mixture to pan and bake for 18 minutes.

Garlic Edamame

Ingredients

  • Frozen edamame
  • Garlic powder
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Minced Garlic

Instructions

  1. Defrost edamame in a pan on medium heat (sesame oil optional).
  2. Add olive oil and seasons to edamame and cook till browned.
  3. Add soy sauce and garlic to pan and cook until desired appearance.

Homemade Cheesy Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Cheese (any amount desired)
  • Any seasoning preferred
  • Egg wash

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Combine ingredients in a bowl, but do not overwork the dough.
  3. Place dough onto sheet and add egg wash and salt.
  4. Bake for 22 minutes.

Spicy Pretzels

Ingredients

  • One bag pretzel sticks
  • 3/4 cup canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 225 degrees.
  2. Combine oil and seasonings in a bowl.
  3. Add pretzels and mixture to gallon ziplock bag and shake.
  4. Let bag sit for one hour, flipping bag halfway through.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes.
