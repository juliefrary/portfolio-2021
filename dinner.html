Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup carrots, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, thinly sliced
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in pan over medium-high heat. Saute onion in butter for about 5 minutes.
  2. Whisk 1/4 cup melted butter and flour together in a large saucepan over medium-low heat for about 3-4 minutes. Add milk if needed.
  3. Pour milk into flour mixture while whisking. Stir chicken stock into milk mixture. Bring to a simmer, cook for about 20 minutes. Add broccoli, carrots, onion, and celery and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in cheese and add salt and pepper.

Crispy Smashed Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Small, round potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Onion and garlic powder
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Rosemary

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees and boil potatoes in salted water until softened.
  2. Drain potatoes and add to a bowl with rest of ingredients.
  3. Place potatoes on a pan with parchment paper and smash each one with the bottom of a cup.
  4. Cook for about 30 minutes, flipping halfway through.

Gemelli Pasta with Peas

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • Package frozen peas
  • Grated parmesean

Instructions

  1. Boil heavily salted water and add olive oil to pan.
  2. Add onion, garlic, salt, and pepper to pan and saute.
  3. Add wine to pan.
  4. Pour pasta and peas into boiling water.
  5. Set aside pasta water, drain pasta, and add to pan with butter and parmesan.

Jon & Vinny's Vodka Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 small garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons vodka
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb any pasta
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 oz grated parmesan
  • Basil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pan over medium heat and add shallot and garlic. Cook for about 5 minutes, until softened.
  2. Add tomato paste to pan and cook until paste is brick red and starts to caramelize, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add vodka and cook until liquid is mostly evaporated, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add cream and red pepper flakes and stir until blended. Season with salt and pepper and remove from heat.
  5. Cook pasta until al dente and reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  6. Cook pasta until al dente and reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  7. Stir until butter has melted, add parmesan, and serve with basil.

Parmesan Orzo with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • 2-3 handfuls of spinach
  • One package of orzo
  • 2 cups of parmesan
  • Vegetable (or chicken) broth
  • Minced garlic
  • Crushed red pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash and cut asparagus.
  2. Add olive oil, garlic, and crushed red pepper to pan on medium heat.
  3. Add asparagus and spinach to pan.
  4. In separate pan, toast orzo with olive oil and garlic.
  5. Once toasted, add broth to pan and cook orzo.
  6. Drain orzo and add vegetables and parmesan to pan.

Zucchini Boats

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano and salt
  • Mozzarella sticks (string cheese)
  • Marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Cut zucchini in half and scoop out middle.
  3. Drizzle zucchini with olive oil and sprinkle with oregano and salt.
  4. Cut mozzarella sticks in half and add to middle of zucchini.
  5. Top off with marinara and toppings of choice.
  6. Cook for 20 minutes.
