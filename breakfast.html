Chocolate Coconut Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup oats
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coconut flakes
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • Optional: honey, protein powder, PB2

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients in container.
  2. Seal container and place in fridge overnight.
  3. Top with fruit and enjoy.
